About:

Ecosharps Solutions is a small scale medical waste transporter. As part of our services, we collect, transport and transfer to a recycler or disposal facility all your medical, dental and pharmaceutical wastes such as syringes, needles or lancets, glass pipettes, used test strips, unused medications, over the counter personal care products and other supplies, all safely, environment-friendly, efficiently and at a reasonable cost. Ecosharps Solutions is ideal for your medical waste disposal needs! Doctors’ offices, clinics and other medical facilities can deal with responsible waste disposal conveniently and in a simple way with us. We are always here for all your medical waste pick up services!

Agreements:

Flexible pick up schedules at your convenience, Affordable pricing, know upfront what you’re paying and pay only what is being picked up. To keep you in compliance with medical waste disposal regulations, we provide you with proof of receipt from the transfer station.

Services:

We offer services to the following medical facilities: Dental offices, clinics, Doctor’s offices, hospitals, pharmacies, laboratories, and private individuals.
